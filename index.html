Portugal

I chose to do my website on Portugal, reason being, that is where I was born. It is a nice looking place and it is very warm.I lived in Portugal for the first 2 years of my life and I’ve visited a couple times when I got a bit older.

Portugal is one of the warmest countrys in Europe. In the main land of Portugal the temperature is around 15°C on average in the north and in the south it averages around 18°C, so if you enjoy warm weather and nice scenery you may enjoy a trip to Portugal. If you wish to visit Portugal you will need to convert your Pounds into Euros, one Euro is approximately £0.89
